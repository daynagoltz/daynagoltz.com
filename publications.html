Currently working on...

Breast Reconstruction Database Curation
I am on a team of USF medical students and plastic surgery residents working to curate a retrospective and prospective database of post-mastectomy breast reconstruction surgeries for further research purposes.
Status: collecting data and writing abstracts/manuscripts.

The Gut Microbiome’s Role in Neurodegenerative Disease
I am working with USF’s Center for Microbiome Research on data analysis comparing the incidence of neurodegenerative disease in older adults with the presence of specific microorganisms.
Status: analyzing data and writing manuscripts

Risk of Osteoporosis in Breast Cancer Patients
I am working to determine the risk of osteoporosis in breast cancer patients under age 65 using Hounsfield Units at the L1 vertebra on PET-CT scans.
Status: collecting data
